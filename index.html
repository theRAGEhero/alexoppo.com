About me

I consider myself an inventor. I’ve invented a smart back panel to prevent back pain. It has an open hardware license, so it’s free to copy and use.

I love to write, that’s the main reason why I opened a blog my first time. You can find some short Italian novels.

I’m a tailor and a backpacker designer. Here you can see some backpacks made by me.

I studied Information and Technology at high school. I had to repeat one year, I ended up leaving high school.

I worked as a system administrator for a big company, then I decided that I was wasting my life and I went back to study IT.

The world has something wrong: I decided to look at the past to be able to make the right step in the present, so I studied History at Università degli studi di Milano.

I love the Internet World and the sharing philosophy. I love blockchain (the technology), it makes easy what otherwise would impossible: my History thesis is about blockchains and you can download it here: Blockchain: decentralization of money, power and the state

I’m very interested by the opportunity that technology offers in therm of democratization of the entire society, that’s why I created democracyroutes.com

Oh, I also write on my old blog www.9minuti.it and I do online chats with university professors, writers, politicians, etc.
